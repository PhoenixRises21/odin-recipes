Eggs and Avocado Breakfast

Description

This avocado egg bake is simple and fun to make. I had these clever breakfast treats at a brunch gathering. The recipe is easy to make for two people and can be doubled or tripled for a crowd. I use a muffin tin to hold each avocado half when making multiple servings.

Ingredients

  • Whole Avocado, Halved and Pitted
  • Two Whole Eggs
  • Pinch of Cayeene Pepper
  • Salt and Ground Black Pepper to Taste
  • 1/4 Cup of Cooked Crumbled Bacon
  • Fresh Chopped Chives

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Place each avocado half in a ramekin. Crack 1 egg into each avocado half; season with cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Place the ramekins on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until egg is cooked through, about 15 minutes. Sprinkle each avocado with crumbled bacon and chives.
  4. ENJOY!
